How it works

  1. 01

    Install the extension and click the icon in your toolbar.

  2. 02

    Add an API key. Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic Claude — your choice.

  3. 03

    Type what you’re looking for in plain English.

  4. 04

    Get back the exact bookmark you were thinking of.

What it does

  • Natural language search

    Type “that React performance article from last year” and it finds it — not just keyword matching, but actual understanding of what you mean.

  • Works with your existing bookmarks

    No importing, no syncing, no new folder structure required. Uses the bookmarks already saved in your browser.

  • Instant suggestions as you type

    Matching bookmarks surface immediately while you type, before the AI response even arrives.

  • Your choice of AI model

    Gemini, OpenAI, or Claude. Bring your own API key and switch providers any time from settings.

Privacy

Your API keys are stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local. Queries are sent directly to the AI provider you select — Google, OpenAI, or Anthropic. Nothing passes through a third-party server. Your bookmarks never leave your browser.
